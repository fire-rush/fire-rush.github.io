Fire Rush Project

Introducing Fire Rush

The new robot Fire Rush has to save a student who is trapped in a burning forest. The student and a group of friends were doing research about forest fires in North America for the environmental college's CBT when they were caught off guard by the fire. Firefighters and the environmental police arrived in a few minutes, but due to the size of the disaster, they send a robot to rescue the students.
